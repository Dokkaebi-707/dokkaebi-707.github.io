1V1 VR

A dream of mine that I've been working on the hardest and the longest. 1V1 VR is a multiplayer VR FPS game that focuses on tactical decisions in pure skill based combat in CQB areas ranging from the back room of a hardware store to the roof of a Korean night club. Featuring a self-made ranking algorithm for finding opponents truly within one's skill level, a progression system linked to user accounts with a built-in shop system (fully controlled by a database), and even co-op modes to play with your friends and complete unique challenges with every mission.

All throughout middle school, and even now, I have always wanted my own game like this, but not just for the sake of creating it. It was because there was nothing like it, and that's exactly what players wanted.

Showcases

Below are just a few screenshots of the art I created for the game, including character model design as well as weapon models.

AKS-74U Weapon Model

The icon used for the default skinned AKS-74U in 1V1 VR. Modelled and rigged entirely in Blender along with a single material texture.

Golden AKS-74U Skin

The icon used for the golden AKS-74U in 1V1 VR. This one in particular is special because the shader used for the gold material was written by me for a certain shine that I wanted which was not available with the default Unity shader.

Default Player Model

The default player model concept used in 1V1 VR. Fully rigged and modelled in Blender, it has the ability to use multiple unique headgear pieces which allows for further customization besides uniform skins in-game.

Unique Beta Skin Concept

The first concept I made for a unique Beta Tester skin for 1V1 VR. Utilizing the efficieny of a single material texture, adding decals to the texture as well as changing certain colors and patterns made the overall skin look fantastic and exclusive for those who received it.

Silver 5 Rank

The icon used for the rank "Silver 5" in 1V1 VR. All icons were made to be perfectly scalable at any resolution so that it can be used in many cases (user profile page, glancing at a small overview of a user, or just seeing their rank next to their name in-game).

Diamond 2 Rank

The icon used for the rank "Diamond 2" in 1V1 VR. Following the same idea behind the Silver rank, just another visualization of different material usage.
